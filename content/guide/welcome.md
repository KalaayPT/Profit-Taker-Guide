## What to Expect

This guide is meant to cover the Profit-Taker fight as a whole, from first clears to faster and more optimized runs. It walks through the basics of the fight, the reasoning behind common setups, and the more specialized strategies that matter once you start refining your runs. The guide is also made to be data-friendly and the entire site does not use more than about 160MB of data.

## Who is This Guide For?

This guide is for anyone who wants to understand or improve the Profit-Taker fight. If you are new to the fight, the earlier pages are meant to get you comfortable with the mechanics and the usual build choices. If you already know the basics, the later sections go further into positioning, landing, timing, squad play, and speedrun-focused strategy.

## How to Use This Guide

If you are new, start with `Getting Started`, then move into `Basic Principles` and the build sections. After that, the later pages expand into more specialized topics and optimizations.

If you are already comfortable with the fight, feel free to jump straight to the topic you need. The menus on the left and right are there to help you move through the guide quickly, and all of the expected navigation options like going back and forth after clicking on a link should work.

::note
All the videos in the guide are paused by default, and you can click to play/pause them. All of the videos are also muted by default so you do not need to worry about sound.
::
