This section covers generic builds for frames using Eclipse.
This includes: Volt builds, Mirage builds, Saryn builds, etc.
They are generally tailored to specific primary weapon setups.
Suggested Warframe Arcanes include: Guardian, Avenger, Acceleration, Velocity
Suggested operator Arcanes include: Elevate, Repair, Lockdown, Cloud

## Frame Build

:build{#04SxaSXSv8}

Umbral Intensify should be replaced with Precision Intensify if your Ogris riven doesnt meet the 150% damage breakpoint.

3 Tau Strength shards, 2 Tau Casting speed

Primed Sure Footed is not necessary due to Negate on Wyrm and faster run time compared to Chroma resulting in less adds.

You must be on the Unairu focus school with Stone Skin passive unlocked in order to proc Primary Bulwark.

## Weapon Builds

### Primary (Kuva Ogris)

:build{#hxNQtu4bAj}

A Riven is mandatory to onetick pylons with Vigorous Swap. The damage % value on Kuva Ogris must be above 150%, if it is lower you need to replace Umbral Intensify with Precision Intensify.

### Melee (Balla)

:build{url="https://arsenyx.com/builds/V6XMMB5aRZ"}

- Rank 0 Melee Exposure is just fine on most builds.
- Rank >0 Exodia Contagion is not necessary.
- The Zaw is Balla + Peye + Vargeet II Jai. If you have a different strike, see **[How to Build A Zaw](/codex/creating-builds.html#zaw-part-setups)** for making a Zaw with a different strike.
- Attack speed is entirely outsourced to the Speed ability.
- A riven can enable oneshotting more elements on Contagion’s damage over time instances.

### Archgun (Velocitus)

:build{url="https://arsenyx.com/builds/xbpdA5bcmK"}

- Riven is mandatory for consistent yellow crits and damage breakpoint for oneshotting legs.
- Use uncharged shots for higher speed and reliability. Volt shield is mandatory.
- Fire Rate mods (up to a total of 90%) can be added depending on riven stats. Refer to Riven Guide for detailed information.
